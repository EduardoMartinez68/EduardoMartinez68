🌟 Welcome to PLUS

A modern, minimalist, and elegant ERP

PLUS is an ERP system inspired by platforms like Odoo, but specifically designed to meet the real needs of Mexico and Latin America.

Built for lawyers and law firms, PLUS solves everyday challenges:

  • ✅ Client management
  • ✅ Case organization
  • ✅ Document handling
  • ✅ Billing
  • ✅ Scheduling
  • ✅ Reporting

All within a clear, professional, and user-friendly interface.

🧠 How did PLUS start?

I wanted to use Odoo to build an ERP for law firms in Mexico. My initial thought was:

“This is going to be a piece of cake.”

Spoiler: It wasn’t 😅

I ran into countless incompatibilities, couldn’t understand other developers’ code 👨🏻‍💻, and every time an error popped up, I had to restore entire files 🤯.

The result? I never even managed to rename the "Projects" module to "Cases" ⚖️… 😂

✨ So I made a decision:

I took a deep breath...
And decided to build my own ERP ENGINE.

With blackjack and hookers? 🃏🍸
Well, not exactly…

More like:

  • 🧼 Clean code
  • 🇲🇽 Designed for Mexican businesses
  • 🧩 Simple customization
  • 💡 Intuitive interface
  • 👶 Ideal even for beginner developers
In PLUS, renaming an app is as easy as a single click.
Adding dependencies or customizing modules is quick and painless.

💬 Personal Note

I have absolutely nothing against Odoo — in fact, I admire it a lot! 👏

It’s an impressive and well-designed platform, built in my favorite language: Python 🐍❤️.

But like many developers, whenever I use a tool, I think: "I could make this better (or at least, more my way)" 😅

Not necessarily better for everyone — just better for me and for other nerds like me who want to help others in this way.

That’s how this project was born: not as a "competitor" to Odoo, but as a simpler, more focused alternative, built from scratch with love. Designed so even inexperienced developers can build their own business apps with ease.
